Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is Ultramarathon Connect?

Ultramarathon Connect is an online platform for discovering, preparing, and connecting with ultramarathons worldwide. We provide tools to explore races, share experiences, and connect with fellow runners.

How do I register for an ultramarathon?

Browse our Race Directory to find a race and follow the registration link provided in the race details.

Account and Membership

Do I need an account to use Ultramarathon Connect?

No, but creating an account unlocks additional features such as posting in the community forums and saving your favorite races.

How do I create an account?

Click on Register in the navigation menu and fill out the registration form to create an account.

Races

How often is the race directory updated?

Our team updates the race directory regularly to ensure you have access to the latest events and details.

Can I suggest a race to be added to the directory?

Yes! Feel free to contact us via the Contact Us page to suggest a race for inclusion in our directory.

Support

How can I contact support?

If you have questions or need help, you can email us at ultramarathonconnect@gmail.com or use the Contact Us page.

What if I encounter a technical issue?

Please contact our support team with a detailed description of the issue, and we will resolve it as soon as possible.
