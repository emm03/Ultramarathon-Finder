Ultra Weekend Recap: June 21–22, 2025

Summer heat kicked into full gear across the ultrarunning world this weekend, as runners battled sun, hills, and long miles in a series of scenic and challenging events.

🌲 Broken Arrow Skyrace – Olympic Valley, California

  • Distances: 46 k, 23 k, 11 k, VK
  • Terrain: Rocky ridgelines, snowfields, alpine roads
  • Weather: Alpine sun with snow at higher elevations
  • 46 k winners: Eli Hemming (3:50:48, new course record), Jennifer Lichter (4:42:32), Helen Mino Faukner (4:43:08), Daniella Moreno (4:43:48) :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
  • Broken Arrow Ascent (VK event): Christian Allen (23:49), Anna Gibson (27:32), qualifying for Mountain Running World Cup :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
  • Official Site

The tough conditions forced high-elevation challenges, including a lightning delay and snow, but elite athletes still smashed records.

That wraps up the June 21–22 ultramarathon weekend! From mountains to monuments, runners showed grit and spirit across the globe.
