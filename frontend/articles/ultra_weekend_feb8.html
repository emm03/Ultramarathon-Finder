Ultra Weekend Recap — February 8–9

By Ultramarathon Connect • 02/10/2025

The second weekend of February delivered desert heat, record-chasing performances, and one of the most competitive ultras of the early season. Here's your recap of the key races that happened February 8–9, 2025:

🏜️ Black Canyon Ultras — Mayer, Arizona

Dates: February 8–9, 2025
Distances: 50K & 100K
Course: Point-to-point desert trail along the Black Canyon Trail
Official Website

🌲 Algonquin 50K — Pocomoke State Forest, Maryland

Date: February 8, 2025
Distance: 50K
Terrain: Fast, flat trails through pine forest and wetlands
Official Website

The Algonquin 50K is a grassroots favorite in the East Coast trail scene. While results had not yet been posted at press time, the race saw over 300 participants toe the line with conditions ranging from crisp to muddy.

Big thanks to all runners, volunteers, and race directors who helped make this weekend another one to remember. We’ll see you on the trails for next week’s recap!
