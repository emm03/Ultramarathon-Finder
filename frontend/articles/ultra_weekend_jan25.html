Ultra Weekend Recap — January 25–26

By Ultramarathon Connect • 01/27/2025

The final weekend of January 2025 saw ultrarunners tackling diverse terrains across the globe. From the rugged trails of Alabama to the scenic routes of Florida, here's a recap of the notable events and results:

Mountain Mist 50K – Huntsville, Alabama

Date: January 25, 2025
Distance: 50K
Full Results

The Mountain Mist 50K challenged runners with its technical trails and elevation changes. The event continues to be a staple in the southeastern ultrarunning community.

In the Woods 30A 50K – Santa Rosa Beach, Florida

Date: January 25, 2025
Distance: 50K
Full Results

Runners enjoyed the scenic coastal trails of Santa Rosa Beach. The event provided a mix of challenging terrain and beautiful views.

This Ain't Yo Momma's 50K & Trail Marathon – Middleburg, Florida

Date: January 26, 2025
Distances: 50K, Marathon
Full Results

This event offered runners a chance to test their limits on the trails of Middleburg. The challenging course was met with enthusiasm by participants.

Running Tribe Ultramarathon – Australia

Date: January 25–26, 2025
Distances: 40M, 50M
Full Results

Down under, the Running Tribe Ultramarathon saw participants pushing through the Australian terrain. The event highlighted the global nature of ultrarunning.

Gut Sutthausen Ultramarathon – Osnabrück, Germany

Date: January 26, 2025
Distance: Ultramarathon
Full Results

In Germany, runners took on the Gut Sutthausen Ultramarathon, embracing the European winter conditions and showcasing endurance and determination.

Congratulations to all the runners who participated in these events! Your dedication and perseverance continue to inspire the ultrarunning community.
