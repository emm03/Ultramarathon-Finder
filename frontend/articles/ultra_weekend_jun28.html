Ultra Weekend Recap: June 28–29, 2025

This weekend featured some standout performances in iconic races from California to South Dakota, with course records falling and tough conditions testing veterans and newcomers alike.

🏁 Western States 100 – Sierra Nevada, California

  • Distance: 100.2 mi (161 km), 18k ft climbing, 23k ft descent
  • Weather: Highs near 100 °F under summer sun
  • Men’s winner: Caleb Olson – 14:11:00 (second‑fastest ever) 🥇
  • Women’s winner: Abby Hall – 16:37:16 (4th fastest, post-injury comeback)
  • Notable: Kilian Jornet ran a strong third, just eight minutes behind Olson
  • Western States Official Site

🌲 Wy’East Wonder 50‑mile & 50k – Parkdale, Oregon

  • Distances: 50 mi and 50 km, high-desert terrain
  • Women’s 50‑mile: Sarah Allaben – 7:36:19 (new course record by >16 min)
  • Men’s 50‑mile: Stephen Snazuk – 7:56:26; Jason Schroeder second, Brian Kelly third
  • Women’s 50k: Sarah Reiter – 4:20:55 (course record, female & 3rd overall)
  • Men’s 50k: Nick Hirdt – 3:55:05; Jonathan Gendron & Joseph Sortor rounded the podium
  • Wy'East Wonder Info

🇨🇦 Tackle the Toad 50k – Nelson, British Columbia

  • Distance: 50 km trail race, second edition
  • Men’s podium: Craig Fowler – 4:47:28; Jonatan Billiau – 5:17:46; James Miles – 5:33:05
  • Women’s podium: Kiah Wheeler – 5:22:53 (also 3rd overall); Jasmine Allchurch – 6:11:42; Jennifer Schaub – 6:12:47
  • Event Page

🏕️ Black Hills 100 – Sturgis, South Dakota

  • Distance: 108 mi; rugged prairie and terrain
  • Men’s winner: Walker Yane – 22:01:47; Caleb Yokom – 22:29:06; Thomas Kjerengtroen – 25:23:31
  • Women’s winner: Mia Rucoba – 30:23:24; Kelsie Sibbald – 33:10:43; Katie Kavanaugh – 33:33:48
  • Official Site

Other races included: Hellz Bellz Ultra (WA), Vulture Vista Ultra (IN), Wings of the West Urban Ultra (FL), Eastern Legend (TX), Hollis Hills Farm Revival (NC), and more.

Congratulations to all finishers this weekend amid heat and mountain challenges! Catch full result lists on UltraRunning Magazine.
