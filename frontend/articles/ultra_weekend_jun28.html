Ultra Weekend Recap: June 28–29, 2025

This weekend featured some standout performances in iconic races from California to South Dakota, with course records falling and tough conditions tested veterans and newcomers alike.

🏁 Western States 100 – Sierra Nevada, California

  • Distance: 100.2 mi (161 km), 18k ft climbing, 23k ft descent
  • Weather: Highs near 100 °F under summer sun
  • Men’s winner: Caleb Olson – 14:11:00 (second‑fastest ever) 🥇
  • Women’s winner: Abby Hall – 16:37:16 (4th fastest, post-injury comeback)
  • Notable: Kilian Jornet ran a strong third, just eight minutes behind Olson :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
