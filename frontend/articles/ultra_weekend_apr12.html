Ultra Weekend Recap — April 12–13

By Ultramarathon Connect • 04/14/2025

The weekend of April 12–13 brought springtime energy to ultrarunners across the U.S., with a lineup of diverse trail and road events. From California’s hilly terrain to the mossy trails of North Carolina, here are the standout results.

Aravaipa's Dam Good Run – Tempe, Arizona

Date: April 13, 2025
Distance: 50k
Course: Paved and gravel trails along Tempe Town Lake and canals
Weather: Sunny, 78°F
Official Website

This scenic springtime race along the Salt River delivered fast times. Top finishers in the 50k were:

  • 1st: Joseph Platt – 3:54:28
  • 2nd: Eric Denning – 4:11:45
  • 3rd: Lindsey Murray – 4:19:07

Rock Creek 50k – Chattanooga, Tennessee

Date: April 13, 2025
Distance: 50k
Location: Lookout Mountain Trails
Weather: Overcast and cool, 58°F
Official Website

Known for its technical footing and elevation gain, this southeast favorite brought in a strong local field. No official results posted at this time.

Black Mountain Monster – Black Mountain, North Carolina

Date: April 13, 2025
Distance: 12-hour, 24-hour timed event
Course: 5k trail loop through wooded parkland
Weather: Cloudy and misty, 66°F
Official Website

This unique event saw runners logging laps into the night. A community vibe with tough performances across both formats.

With warm weather and fresh legs, April continues to deliver solid racing opportunities. Congrats to all runners who pushed their limits this weekend!
