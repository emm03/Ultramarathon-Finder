Ultra Weekend Recap — March 29–30

By Ultramarathon Connect • 03/31/2025

The final weekend of March 2025 featured a series of challenging ultramarathon events across diverse terrains. Here's a recap of the key races:

🐍 Rattlesnake Dick 50K — Auburn, California

Date: March 29, 2025
Distance: 50K
Course: Technical trails with scenic views
Official Website

Note: Live race tracking and finish line results were provided by UltraLive.net. Detailed results are available on the official website.

🏃 Chicago Lakefront Ultras — Chicago, Illinois

Date: March 29, 2025
Distances: 50K, Half Marathon
Course: Flat and fast course along Lake Michigan
Official Results

Note: The event featured 72 finishers in the 50K and 35 finishers in the Half Marathon. Detailed results are available on the official website.

🏔️ Georgia Death Race — Blairsville, Georgia

Date: March 29, 2025
Distance: Approximately 74 miles
Course: Challenging trail with 16,000 ft of climbing and descent
Official Website

Note: Runners are required to carry a 1 lb railroad spike throughout the race. Detailed results are available on the official website.

🌄 Sleeping Giant Trail Runs — Hamden, Connecticut

Date: March 30, 2025
Distance: 25K
Course: Technical trails with over 3,000 ft of vertical gain
Official Website

Note: The race is considered one of the toughest in the Northeast. Detailed results are available on the official website.

Congratulations to all the runners, volunteers, and organizers who made this weekend's events memorable. Stay tuned for next week's recap!
