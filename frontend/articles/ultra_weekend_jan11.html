Ultra Weekend Recap — January 11–12

By Ultramarathon Connect • 01/13/2025

The second weekend of 2025 featured a variety of ultramarathon events across the globe. Here's a recap of the notable races and results:

No Hands Trail Run – Auburn, California

Date: January 11, 2025
Distances: 50K, 35K, Half Marathon, 8K
Location: American River Overlook Park
Official Website

The No Hands Trail Run offered a range of distances for trail enthusiasts. The 50K race challenged runners with its scenic yet demanding course.

Lost in Miami Ultra – Miami, Florida

Date: January 11, 2025
Distance: 18-Hour Fixed Time Event
Location: 17049 S Dixie Hwy, Palmetto Bay, FL
Official Website

This unique urban ultramarathon challenged participants to navigate through Miami, visiting various landmarks within 18 hours. Runners had the freedom to choose their own routes, making for a strategic and adventurous race.

Aotearoa Ultra Marathon – Banks Peninsula, New Zealand

Date: January 11, 2025
Distance: 53K
Location: Banks Peninsula, Canterbury
Full Results

The Aotearoa Ultra Marathon showcased New Zealand's stunning landscapes, offering runners a challenging 53K course with breathtaking views.

Backyard Ultra Taipei – Taipei, Taiwan

Date: January 11–14, 2025
Format: Last Man Standing
Official Website

This endurance event tested runners' limits with a last-man-standing format, where participants completed 6.7K loops every hour until only one remained.

Congratulations to all the runners who participated in these events! Your dedication and perseverance continue to inspire the ultrarunning community.
